# Purchase credits

PMM Sherpa runs on a credit system. One query (any of the four tools, any client) costs **2 credits**. Credits cover the LLM tokens, the agentic RAG retrieval across 38,000+ chunks, and any web research the advisor pulls in.

## Plans

| Plan | Monthly credits | Price | Topups |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | 10 | $0 | Not available |
| Starter | 200 | $9.99 / mo | $5 for 100, $19 for 500, $39 for 1,200 |

- **Free** resets to 10 credits on the first of each month.
- **Starter** resets to 200 credits on the first of each month.
- **Topup credits** never expire. They sit on top of your monthly allowance and are spent only after the monthly allowance runs out.
- Credits never carry over from month to month on Free or Starter. Topups roll over indefinitely.

## How a query is metered

- `ask_sherpa`: 2 credits per question
- `draft_artifact`: 2 credits per artifact
- `get_feedback`: 2 credits per critique
- `scope_pmm_research`: 2 credits per scoping call

If a tool fails (timeout, infrastructure error), credits are refunded automatically.

## How to buy credits

In the web app:

1. Open [pmmsherpa.com](https://pmmsherpa.com) and sign in.
2. Click your avatar (top right) → **Settings** → **Billing**.
3. Click **Buy credits** and pick a topup pack ($5, $19, or $39).
4. Stripe Checkout opens. Pay with card. Credits land on your account in seconds.

You can also upgrade Free → Starter from the same page. The first monthly allowance is granted immediately on upgrade.

## Where credits apply

Credits are tied to your PMM Sherpa account, not the client. Whether you are calling tools from Claude.ai, Claude Code, ChatGPT, Codex, Gemini CLI, or Antigravity, the same credit balance is debited.

## Troubleshooting

- **"Out of credits" error during a tool call**: open the in-product modal at [pmmsherpa.com](https://pmmsherpa.com) and buy a topup. The next tool call succeeds without restarting your AI client.
- **Receipt not received**: check spam, then [contact support](mailto:hi@pmmsherpa.com). Stripe receipts come from `stripe.com`.
- **Refund**: if a tool call fails, credits are refunded automatically. If you need a payment refund, email support.
